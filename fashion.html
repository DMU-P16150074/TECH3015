FASHION

Clothing can be so important to people when it comes to confidence. I can put my hand up and say it definitely affects mine. Not in a way that I feel unconfident without a good outfit, but on a day when I'm wearing my favourite boots or a dress that I was once told looked good on me makes me feel like Im going to have a successful day...

When describing my wardrobe, I like the say that the majority of the pieces are for comfort, I feel like I have fully mastered the way to dress for comfort as well as having a touch of style. Usually people would just think of wearing big hoodies and gym leggings, which still is a good idea, but don't completely rule out other all the other alternatives. Here are just a few ways I suggest that you can dress for comfort with style...

Nooo not that dreaded word! I imagine for some of you, your wardrobe consists of mainly black, white and maybe some grey. I know because that used to be me... A traditional winter wardrobe solely containing dark colours. I'm testing you to try and step out of your comfort zone and inject a bit of colour into your wardrobe this season...

MY TOP 5 FASHION ITEMS

  • ALWAYS a Little black dress

    • A little black dress makes last minute night outs the easiest! Just chuck one on and you look glam without even trying.

  • White chunky trainers

    • An absolute fave of mine. Big, chunky trainers makes any look cool and on trend.

  • Gold Jewellery

    • Oooo gold hoops, necklaces, rings!! I cannot recommend them more.

  • Leather look trousers

    • If you can find a comfy pair of leather or leather look trousers you've hit the jackpot!

  • Basic crop tops

    • Anyone that knows me says that they never see me without one... I'm bias but I love them, so love them too.
