Hey guys, I’m back! This is my new segment called Student Loan Luxuries. Being a student that lives at home board free, whilst working every weekend, means that I have a bit of cash to spare. Instead of wasting the money on multiple orders on Missguided or Pretty Little Thing, I’d rather save it up and spend it on city-breaks or trips abroad. Travelling has always been something I have thoroughly enjoyed whether it’s a chill, sun holiday with bae or a boozy weekend trip with the girls.

Welcome back to the Student Loan Luxuries segment of my blog. I really hope you enjoyed the last one, I enjoy reminiscing on my travels and rambling on about all the best bits! As you can see from the title, the next travel destination is Dublin, the heart of Ireland. Yet again, the reason for going was another one of my best friend’s birthdays. Because of this the reason for going was ‘a big piss up’, So if you’re looking for more of an educational and a sightseeing view on Dublin you may want to look elsewhere...

Wow I’m getting good at this now, welcome back to yet another Student Loan Luxuries segment! The third and final destination of this segment is Paris, the city of loveee. My boyfriend and I went together at the end of August last year. We had gone for a long weekend and decided to stay in a Disney hotel so that we could spend a few days in Disneyland and then travel out to Paris and spend a full day there...
